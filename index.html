Connected Analytics

Growing up in a country like Nigeria you will understand that HOPE is just a dream for most people. I have always wanted to help and create HOPE where there seems to be none....

I ran a financial services company and during that time I realized that the middle class was becoming nonexistent. On my platform, I saw that people were opening businesses and were not getting customers. We had merchants in very high numbers (at a point over 10,000) with no purchases or customers. This, in turn, shows a declining economy. Businesses are closing shop and running out of business. These were painful moments for me. It was not really about charging and making money, but really how could I help these merchants.

I set out on a mission to solve this problem. I know investors don’t like hearing this but it was never about making money. No, no, no, it was about giving hope to the hopeless and bringing businesses back to life while generating extra income for shoppers.

We set out to connect 10,000 businesses to 10,000,000 customers in 5 years. A very ambitious plan but with the help of my magnificent friends, who believed in the dream, I believed we would make it.

Connected Analytics is operating well today; we have even started increasing from the founding team to add more wonderful people to the team. We want to strive to make our Shareholders happy, excite and get our team pumped up, create a community of friendliness around our partner businesses and customers and finally making everybody in the chain have enough to say success is theirs.

Our company has grown in a very short time into a platform with many products, and ThankUCash being the most popular and successful. However, we will continue to grow and make people, be it businesses, banks or customers grow. We are on a mission and we pray you to join us to make everyone we touch remember us and have a growth-filled life.

I am excited to be running Connected Analytics as CEO today and would not be prouder working with the co-founding team of Madonna Ononobi, Suraj Supekar, and Harshal Gandole. These guys make building a company so much fun.
